Our Profile

Structural Integrity Meets Design

Located in the vibrant heart of Rio de Janeiro, RIO DESIGN CONSTRUCOES PROJETOS E SERVICOS LTDA is a premier construction firm dedicated to the non-residential sector. Under the leadership of Silvio Marcelo Dos Santos Silva, we have established a reputation for executing complex industrial and commercial projects with unwavering precision.

Our firm believes that "design" in construction goes beyond appearance; it is about the intelligent integration of structural systems and operational efficiency. We specialize in navigating the unique logistical and regulatory landscape of Rio, ensuring seamless project delivery from groundbreaking to handover.

Our team consists of veteran engineers and architects committed to safety, sustainability, and innovation. We don't just build edifices; we construct the environments where Rio's business thrives.
